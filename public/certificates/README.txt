CERTIFICATES FOLDER
===================
Place your certificate PDF files here.

For the NPTEL ML Certificate:
  → Save your certificate as: nptel-ml.pdf
  → Full path: public/certificates/nptel-ml.pdf

The ExperienceSection.js references this file at:
  pdfPath: '/certificates/nptel-ml.pdf'

When you click the certification card in the portfolio,
it will open a modal viewer showing this PDF.
